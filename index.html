The name "Inunomics" is derived from "Inu," a nod to the popular Shiba Inu dog breed that has become synonymous with memecoins, and "nomics," indicating economic principles and financial systems. The idea is to provide a singular token that embodies the various attributes and communities of the numerous Inu tokens, such as Shiba Inu, Floki Inu, Akita Inu, and many others. By doing so, Inunomics seeks to simplify the Inu ecosystem, offering a single investment avenue that captures the collective value and excitement of these individual tokens.
